Maze Marauder

About Us

Hi there! This game was developed by Daniel Y, Daniel M, Daniel T, and Pradyumn J, and we are high school students graduating in '24.

3Daniel(s)1Pradyumn Studios presents ... Maze Marauder!

Our game, Maze Marauder, is a unique blend of maze exploration and dungeon fighting. In a race against time, the player must skillfully navigate the sub-zero maze in an effort to gather resources and materials. With twists and turns every direction, compelling boss levels, and fun gameplay, Maze Marauder is a game for the bold :D

Check out the instructions by navigating the menu through the top-left hamburger icon, and you can play the game by clicking on the 'play' button found on the Main Menu page. Good luck adventurer!
